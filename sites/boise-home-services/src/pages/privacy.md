This Privacy Policy describes how Boise Home Services ("the Company", "We", "Us", or "Our") collects, uses, and protects Your information when You use our Website and services.

## Definitions

- **Company** refers to Boise Home Services, Boise, Idaho 83702.
- **Country** refers to: Idaho, United States
- **Website** refers to Boise Home Services, accessible from [https://boisehome.services](https://boisehome.services)
- **Service** refers to the Website, AI chatbot, and the home services provided by the Company.
- **Personal Data** is any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
- **You** means the individual accessing or using the Service.

## Information We Collect

### Contact Form and Estimate Request Data

When You submit a contact form or estimate request, we collect the information You provide, which may include:

- Your name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Project address
- Type of service needed
- Project description and details

This information is used solely to respond to Your inquiry, provide estimates, schedule services, and communicate about Your project.

### AI Chatbot Conversations

Our website may feature an AI-powered chatbot to help answer questions about our services. Chatbot conversation data is:

- Stored temporarily for session continuity (so the chatbot can reference earlier parts of your conversation)
- **Not shared** with third parties for marketing purposes
- Not used to build profiles or target advertising
- Automatically deleted after 30 days

### Analytics Data

We use analytics services to understand how visitors use our Website:

- **Cloudflare Web Analytics**: Privacy-focused analytics that does not use cookies or track individual users. Collects aggregate data about page views, visit duration, and referral sources.
- **Google Analytics** (if enabled): May collect device information, browser type, pages visited, time on site, and general geographic location. Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique users.

### Cookies and Local Storage

Our Website may use:

- **Essential cookies**: Required for basic website functionality (e.g., theme preference)
- **Analytics cookies**: Used by Google Analytics (if enabled) to understand website usage
- **Local storage**: Used to remember Your preferences (e.g., dark/light mode)

You can control cookies through Your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality but will not prevent You from browsing our services.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Respond to Your inquiries and provide estimates
- Schedule and perform home services
- Communicate about Your projects (status updates, scheduling changes)
- Improve our Website and services
- Comply with legal obligations

## Information We Do NOT Collect or Sell

- We do **not** sell Your personal information to third parties
- We do **not** use Your information for targeted advertising
- We do **not** share Your contact information with other businesses for marketing
- We do **not** collect financial information through our Website (payments are handled in person or through secure payment processors)

## Data Retention

- **Contact form submissions and estimate requests**: Retained for up to 2 years to support ongoing customer relationships and warranty records
- **Chatbot conversations**: Automatically deleted after 30 days
- **Analytics data**: Retained according to the analytics provider's policies (Cloudflare retains aggregate data; Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months by default)
- **Project records**: Retained for 7 years to support warranty claims and legal requirements

## Your Rights

Under Idaho law and general privacy principles, You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about You
- **Correction**: Request that we correct inaccurate personal information
- **Deletion**: Request that we delete Your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Opt-out**: Opt out of analytics tracking by using browser settings or privacy extensions

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@boisehome.services.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect Your personal information, including:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure hosting on Cloudflare infrastructure
- Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While We strive to protect Your information, We cannot guarantee absolute security.
